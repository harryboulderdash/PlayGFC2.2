The Gaming For Cancer™ staff is extremely excited to announce the launch of our new and improved website, PlayGFC.com!

PlayGFC offers a competitive environment for Call of Duty™ players and provides them with consistent opportunities to win large cash prizes.

Gaming For Cancer is gaming, but with a mission. Gaming For Cancer donates 15% of all proceeds to nonprofit organizations providing financial support to those fighting Cancer.

Those who participate in Gaming For Cancer’s tournaments take pride in their gaming, as they are proud to game AND help those suffering from Cancer, at the same time.

How can YOU Game For Cancer???

  1. Get yourself a username at PlayGFC.com, it’s FREE and EASY.
  2. Check out our upcoming tournaments and select one at a day and time that works for you.
  3. Get some credits in your GFC bank through our Online Store.
  4. When you have credits purchased select a tournament, click on ‘Join’, give your team a name and add yourself to it in one simple step.
  5. Invite your teammates as needed! You can cover your teammates entry fee when you invite them to the team!
  6. Once logged in, the invited players can see their invites and accept them.
