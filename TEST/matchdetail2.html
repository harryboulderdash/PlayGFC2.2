About Us

  • Configure block

GFC is dedicated to providing the best online e-sports tournament experience possible. We are gamers, and love first and foremost the fun of competitive gaming. We believe we can connect gamers together, run top notch cash prize competitions with fair play on all sides, and while we're at it... help knock cancer offline, one game at a time.

Cancer is our common enemy that has affected people we love and we take the fight to help those who are fighting it seriously. A portion of all money goes directly to fund cancer nonprofit groups doing important work on the battleground. Read about Our Mission for details. We hope you will join us for some world class gaming, compete for cash prizes and be a part of the GFC mission.

We are new and still in our Beta release period, so we are depending on your input to make the site experience great, and we are only at the beginning of building it out. If you see something that you like, or that needs work, please let us know. We'd love to hear any ideas you have and appreciate your help creating more than a gaming site, but a unique gaming community where we play hard, have fun and make a real difference. Welcome to PlayGFC.com!
